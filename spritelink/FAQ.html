Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q:What is Spritelink?
A: In case you didn't look at the front page *rolls eyes*, Spritelink is the connection between freelancers and employers in the Louisville area! It allows you to hire locally, advertise locally, and network locally. Keeping it all in the city.

Q: How do I sign up?
A: You see the button labeled "Get Started"? Yeah, go there.

Q: What is the logo supposed to mean?
A: We just liked it. It was done by one of our freelancers ;)

Q: Is signing up free?
A: Is anything really free? Anyway, yes, signing up is free for customers AND freelancers...so I guess somethings are free.

Q: How do I get paid?
A: You and the employer will agree upon a price for the work done. Keep in mind that Spritelink does take a portion of the sale. It can be done over PayPal, or you can link a bank account so the money will be deposited into the account directly.

Q: Who do I contact if I have any questions?
A: We have a customer service line listed on the "Contact Us" page. Questions can be directed to them and them only since we don't want to deal with it.

Q: How do I get on the top freelancers list?
A: You have to become the top freelancer, duh. The top freelancers are conducted by a very sophisticated equation to determine their positions, i.e., how they are rated by the employer, how many people save the freelancer's business card, and how many times they are hired.

Q: Do I have to be both a customer and freelancer?
A: Power to you if you are, but no you do not have to be both.

Q: What kind of freelancers are even on Spritelink?
A: Any and all kinds of freelancers are on our site. The only requirements are that you live in the Louisville area.

Have other questions? Visit the "Contact Us" page to send in your questions. We can't promise it won't be a sarcastic answer, but we can promise an answer!
