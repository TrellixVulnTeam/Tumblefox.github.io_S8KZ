Spritelink Is Secure

Our secure servers ensure safe transactions for every user. In addition, we aim to look after both the customers and the freelancers. Spritelink holds payment during a transaction until the freelancer has finished the job and the customer is satisfied. In other words, we want to make sure the customer can be refunded and the freelancer will be paid.

We Have Diverse Payment Options

Customers can pay with Paypal, a credit card, or their Bitcoin wallet. We will hold the payment and release the funds for only a 5% fee. With these options, we introduce a progressive and modern ways to hire a freelancer. We also keep records of all transactions for customers and freelancers to review.

We Promote Community Growth

Spritelink provides a platform for freelancers of all walks of life, promoting diversity in Louisville's independent sector. We aim to create an environment where any person with valuable skills or goods can be patronized, opening the door for customers to find Louisville's freelancers and connect with them as patrons and as neighbors.
